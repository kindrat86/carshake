Car Lease Handover Checklist

A checklist for documenting vehicle condition at lease start and end.

How to use: work through each section in order. Don't skip steps — the order matters.

Lease start

  • Mileage recorded
  • All damage photographed and noted
  • Fuel level
  • Keys and fobs count

During lease

  • Mileage tracked monthly
  • Service records maintained
  • Any damage documented promptly

Lease end

  • Final mileage recorded
  • Condition compared to start photos
  • Wear-and-tear vs damage assessment
  • Disposition fee confirmed

Why this checklist exists

This checklist was built for teams handling vehicle condition app. Each item addresses a common failure mode we've seen in real workflows. Skipping a step doesn't save time — it creates rework, disputes, or missed signals downstream.

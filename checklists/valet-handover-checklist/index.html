Do I really need to do all 10 steps every time?

The full routine takes under 3 minutes. Even doing just steps 1, 6, and 8 — scan, QR handover, and re-scan — covers you for the vast majority of disputes. The other steps are risk-reducers: mileage protects against joyriding claims, valuables against theft, and the valet ticket establishes custody.

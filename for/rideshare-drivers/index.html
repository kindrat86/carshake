Rideshare and delivery drivers spend more hours on the road than almost anyone, and their vehicles are their livelihood. A disputed scratch or dent means lost income while the car is being repaired — and without proof of when the damage occurred, the driver usually pays. CarShake gives drivers a free 60-second routine: scan every time you park at a busy pickup zone, in a garage, or anywhere your car is out of sight. The timestamped, GPS-verified record proves where and when your vehicle was clean — and where and when it was not. For rideshare drivers on tight margins, 60 seconds of scanning at handover is the cheapest insurance you will ever buy.

Beyond individual protection, rideshare drivers who scan consistently build a documented vehicle history that is valuable for resale, lease returns, and insurance audits. Showing a buyer or dealer timestamped condition reports from every major parking event removes the negotiation leverage that comes from undocumented wear-and-tear claims. Fleet owners with multiple vehicles can manage scans across their entire fleet from a single dashboard, track incident rates by driver and location, and use the data to negotiate lower insurance premiums with evidence of a documented loss-prevention protocol. For independent drivers, CarShake is completely free — no subscription, no per-scan fee, no hidden costs. The only investment is 60 seconds of your time before you walk away from your car.

Hospitality Valet Damage Prevention

How hotels, resorts, and hospitality venues use CarShake to eliminate valet damage disputes and improve guest satisfaction scores.

Why hospitality valet damage is a brand problem

A single valet damage incident at a 4-star hotel can erase the goodwill from a flawless check-in experience. TripAdvisor and Google Reviews amplify the damage: "The valet scratched my car and the hotel denied it" is a review that stays visible for years. Hotels that adopt damage-proof handover protocols reduce valet-related complaints by an estimated 80% and protect their online reputation.

The pre/post-handover protocol

Leading hospitality brands are implementing a scan-before/scan-after protocol: the valet attendant and guest each scan the vehicle using CarShake at handover and pickup. Both parties receive a QR-coded receipt confirming the car's condition at each transition point. This creates a transparent, mutually acknowledged chain of custody that eliminates he-said/she-said disputes before they start.

Insurance premium impact

Hotels with documented damage-proof protocols report 40-60% fewer garage-keepers liability claims and qualify for reduced insurance premiums. The protocol documentation itself becomes a risk-mitigation asset during underwriting.

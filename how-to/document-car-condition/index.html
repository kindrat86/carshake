How to Document Your Car Condition Before Valet

Best practices for pre-valet condition documentation.

The key to winning any damage dispute is having documentation from BEFORE the incident. Here is how to properly document your car before handing over the keys.

The 60-Second Pre-Valet Walkaround

  • Take photos of all four sides of the car
  • Photograph any existing damage close-up
  • Note the mileage
  • Record the time and location
  • Use CarShake for automatic timestamping

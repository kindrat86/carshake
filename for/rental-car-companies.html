CarShake for Rental Car Companies

CarShake documents a rental car's condition at pick-up and at return with timestamped, mutually confirmed scans. Damage claims stop being arguments about who is remembering correctly and become a comparison of two records both parties signed off on.

Why rental operators use CarShake

Rental damage claims are painful in both directions. Charge a customer for a scratch they insist was already there and you earn a chargeback, a one-star review, and sometimes a regulator complaint. Miss real damage at return and the repair comes out of your margin. Both failures trace to the same gap: no shared record of condition at pick-up.

CarShake closes that gap. The customer confirms the pick-up condition with a QR handshake before driving off, so the return comparison starts from evidence both sides already accepted. Every photo carries a GPS stamp, a server timestamp, and a SHA-256 hash — nothing can be backdated or swapped after the fact.

Where it fits your operation

Use it at the counter for walk-up rentals, in the lot for contactless pick-ups, or at return lanes. Because it is a browser app, customers do not install anything, and agents can run it on any phone or tablet already at the counter.

How a CarShake handover works

  1. Scan before handover. A guided walk-around captures 8 angles of the vehicle in about 60 seconds. Every photo is GPS-stamped, server-timestamped, and SHA-256 hashed, so nobody can backdate or alter it.
  2. Confirm with a QR handshake. The other party scans a QR code and acknowledges the recorded condition. Both sides now hold the same evidence.
  3. Re-scan on return. A second walk-around captures the vehicle's condition at hand-back.
  4. Compare. AI-assisted comparison lines up the before and after scans and flags anything new. If there is a dispute, either side can export a court-ready PDF evidence report.

Frequently asked questions

How is this different from the paper damage diagram on the rental agreement?

A paper diagram records what an agent noticed in the moment, with no timestamps and no photos. A CarShake scan records 8 photographed angles, timestamped and confirmed by the customer.

Does it work for contactless pick-up?

Yes. The customer scans the car themselves from their own phone browser and confirms the condition before driving off.

Can customers use CarShake against unfair claims?

Yes — and that is good for honest operators. When both sides hold the same record, unfair claims in either direction collapse quickly.

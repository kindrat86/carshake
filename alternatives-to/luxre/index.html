When to choose each

Both Luxre and CarShake have legitimate use cases. The right choice depends on what you are trying to protect against. Luxre and CarShake overlap in some areas but solve fundamentally different problems for vehicle owners. Understanding the distinction up front saves you from paying for the wrong tool after a damage dispute has already started.

Use Luxre if you

  • Need the specific feature set Luxre is optimized for, and already have a separate damage-documentation workflow in place.
  • Are already locked into the Luxre ecosystem and switching costs outweigh the benefits of a purpose-built damage-proof tool.
  • Have a use case where Luxre clearly outperforms generalist apps (for example, a niche integration or a regional feature CarShake does not cover).

Use CarShake if you

  • Park with valets, rent cars, or hand your keys to anyone and need court-admissible before/after condition proof in under 60 seconds.
  • Want a free tool that scans your car at 8 standard angles, time-stamps every photo, and generates a QR-coded handover receipt the valet signs.
  • Have been burned by a damage dispute in the past (the average valet claim runs $2,100 and a single disputed charge costs $500+ in time, deductibles, and stress).

Use both if you

  • Already rely on Luxre for its core function and want to layer CarShake on top purely for the pre/post-handover damage-proof workflow. The two tools are not mutually exclusive — many drivers run a generalist app alongside CarShake specifically for the valet and rental-car handover moment.

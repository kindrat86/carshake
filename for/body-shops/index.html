Body shops see damaged cars every day — which is precisely why a documented intake record matters. Without a timestamped pre-repair scan, a customer can claim the shop caused damage that was already present. CarShake gives your estimators the same 8-angle AI scan that protects drivers: capture every panel before work begins, generate a QR-confirmed handover receipt, and rescan at completion. The before-and-after comparison also serves as a quality-assurance record, showing the customer exactly what was repaired and proving the work was done. For shops that bill insurance, the timestamped evidence package speeds up claims and reduces adjuster pushback.

Shops can also use the scan protocol as a marketing differentiator. Customers who receive a timestamped before-and-after report showing every panel repaired, with GPS and time metadata, are significantly more likely to leave positive reviews and recommend the shop. The transparency builds trust in an industry where trust is the single biggest barrier to customer retention. For insurance-direct-repair programs, the evidence package satisfies the documentation requirements many carriers demand, reducing cycle time on supplements and re-inspections. And because CarShake stores every scan immutably, the records serve as a permanent quality log — useful for training, process improvement, and defending against warranty claims that surface months after the repair.

What Is Valet Parking Liability?

Understanding who is responsible when a valet damages your car.

Valet parking liability determines who pays when your car is damaged while in a valet's custody. Understanding this helps you navigate disputes effectively.

The Legal Principle

When you hand your keys to a valet, a bailment is created. The valet company assumes responsibility for the vehicle while it is in their custody. They are legally liable for damage caused by their employees.

Common Defenses Valet Companies Use

  • Claiming damage was pre-existing (defeated by pre-photos)
  • Arguing the damage is under their deductible
  • Blaming another customer or third party
  • Claiming you signed a waiver (often unenforceable)

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a valet company waive liability?

Liability waivers in valet parking are often unenforceable because they attempt to waive negligence. The valet company remains responsible for damage caused by their employees' negligence.

What CarShake actually is

CarShake is a free valet-damage-proof and vehicle-handover app. It scans and time-stamps a car's condition before and after handover, giving drivers, valet operators, and rental fleets court-admissible proof to defeat false damage claims. The core workflow takes 60 seconds: open the app, run a guided 8-angle walkaround scan, generate a QR handover receipt the operator acknowledges at the moment of transfer, run the same scan when you get the car back, and review the before/after diff that isolates any new damage.

CarShake is a consumer-and-operator valet-damage-proof handover app — not a B2B insurance damage-detection API. If you are an individual driver, a frequent traveler, a rental car customer, a luxury or exotic car owner, or a fleet operator looking to protect your vehicles at the handover moment, we built this for you.

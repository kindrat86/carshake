How the CarShake + Usaa workflow works

Usaa policyholders get into damage disputes the same way every other driver does — a valet, a rental return, a parking lot, a dealer service visit — and the same gap in evidence sinks their claim. CarShake fills that gap with time-stamped, geo-located before/after scans that Usaa adjusters can evaluate directly. There is no formal API integration required: CarShake produces documentation that fits cleanly into Usaa's existing claims process.

Setup steps

  1. Download CarShake (free). The free tier includes 3 scans — enough to document a full round-trip valet or rental handover and prove the workflow fits your life.
  2. Run the pre-handover scan. Before you hand your keys to a valet, rental counter, or service advisor, open CarShake and run the guided 8-angle scan. Takes under 60 seconds. Every photo is time-stamped and geo-located automatically.
  3. Generate the QR receipt. Have the attendant scan the QR handover receipt to acknowledge the vehicle condition at the moment of transfer. This step alone prevents most later denials.
  4. Run the post-handover scan. When you get the car back, run the same 8-angle scan. CarShake produces a before/after diff highlighting any new damage.
  5. Attach to your Usaa claim. Export the diff with timestamps and geo-data and include it in your Usaa claim filing. Adjusters accept time-stamped photo evidence — which is exactly what CarShake produces.

Data flow

The data flow is intentionally simple and does not require any plumbing between CarShake and Usaa:

  • CarShake captures: 8-angle photo set, timestamp, GPS coordinates, and operator QR acknowledgement — all bundled into a single condition record.
  • CarShake computes: a side-by-side diff between pre- and post-handover scans that isolates any damage that appeared during the handover window.
  • You submit: export the diff as a PDF or shareable link and attach it to your Usaa claim, dispute letter, or police report. The format is designed to be read by human adjusters, not parsed by a system.
  • Usaa resolves: your adjuster gets a clean, time-stamped, geo-located evidence packet that shows exactly when and where the damage occurred. This is the documentation that moves a claim from disputed to resolved.

How the Free Car Scanner Works

Open CarShake on any smartphone browser — no app download needed. Walk around your car while the camera captures 8 angles: front, rear, both sides, and all four wheel corners. The AI timestamps each photo with GPS coordinates and generates a SHA-256 hash for tamper-proof verification. Total time: about 60 seconds.

When you return, scan again. CarShake's AI compares every angle pixel by pixel and flags any new scratches, dents, or scrapes. You get a side-by-side report with timestamps and GPS data — free, every time. Learn more about the 3-Stop Protocol →

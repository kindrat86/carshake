What to compare in a vehicle inspection app

Inspection apps all take photos. Disputes are rarely lost over photo quality — they are lost over trust and acknowledgment. When you compare Damage iD, CarShake, or any other tool in this category, check four things:

  1. Timestamp authority. Are captures stamped by a server the parties don't control, or does the record rely on device time?
  2. File integrity. Can anyone prove the photos weren't edited or replaced later? CarShake hashes every capture with SHA-256.
  3. Mutual confirmation. Does the other party acknowledge the record at handover? CarShake's QR handshake makes the record bilateral — the single biggest factor in how quickly disputes end.
  4. Friction. Who has to install what? CarShake runs in the browser, so the other party never needs an app.

The Story Behind CarShake

About CarShake

From a $4,200 valet damage lesson to a global vehicle handover platform protecting thousands of drivers.

"I built CarShake because my car got damaged at valet — and I had zero proof."

December 2023. I handed my keys to a hotel valet. When the car came back, there was $4,200 in damage. The valet manager shrugged: "How do we know it wasn't like that when you dropped it?"

He was right. I had nothing. No photos. No witness. No proof. I paid out of pocket.

That night, CarShake was born. A system that makes valet damage disputes impossible to deny.

Our Mission

We're building a world where no driver pays for damage they didn't cause. Every scan makes parking more accountable. Every QR handover receipt creates an irrefutable chain of custody.

The CarShake 3-Stop Protocol™

Three minutes. Three actions. Zero arguments.

1

Scan Before Valet

Open CarShake. Walk around your car. 8 timestamped, GPS-verified photos cover every angle. Takes 60 seconds.

2

QR Handover Receipt

Show the attendant your QR code. They scan it. Both parties digitally confirm the car's condition.

3

Post-Retrieval Scan

When you get your car back, scan again. AI compares every angle, flags every new scratch, dent, or scrape.

Key terms, defined

Valet damage proof
Timestamped photographic evidence of a vehicle's condition before and after a valet or rental handover.
Court-admissible timestamp
A tamper-evident time record that can be used as evidence in a dispute or claim.
Vehicle handover receipt

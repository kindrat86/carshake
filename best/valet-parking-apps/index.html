The 5 Best Valet Parking Apps for Booking and Protection

Valet parking apps fall into two categories: booking apps (find and reserve parking) and protection apps (document car condition). Here is how they compare.

1. SpotHero 🏆 Editor's Choice

Booking and price comparison

Pricing: Free

Verdict: Best for finding and reserving parking spots in advance. No damage documentation or handover features.

2. ParkWhiz

Guaranteed reservations

Pricing: Free

Verdict: Similar to SpotHero with guaranteed-reservation parking. Event parking is a strength. No damage protection.

3. BestParking

Real-time price comparison

Pricing: Free

Verdict: Excellent price-comparison search across garages and lots. No booking guarantee or handover protection.

4. CarShake

Damage protection at any valet

Pricing: Free (3 scans/mo)

Verdict: The only app in the category purpose-built for the handover documentation gap. Does not book parking — it protects you at whatever valet you use.

5. Uber / Lyft

Avoiding valet altogether

Pricing: Varies by ride

Verdict: The best valet damage protection is not parking. For short stays where parking costs more than a round-trip ride, skip the valet entirely.

Can I use SpotHero and CarShake together?

Yes. Use SpotHero or ParkWhiz to find and book parking, then use CarShake at the valet stand to document your car's condition. They serve completely different purposes and complement each other.

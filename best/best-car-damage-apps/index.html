Selection criteria

Not every app in the car damage [2026] space is built for the same job. When we evaluate apps in this category for real-world damage-dispute use, we look at six concrete criteria:

  • Purpose-built for the handover moment. Does the app explicitly support before/after scans tied to a specific transfer event, or is it just a general photo gallery?
  • Guided, consistent coverage. Does it walk you through a fixed set of angles so you do not miss high-risk zones like bumper corners and wheels?
  • Time-stamped and geo-located photos. Are timestamps and GPS coordinates baked into the photo metadata, not just added as text overlays a user could fake?
  • Before/after diffing. Can it actually compare two scans and surface new damage, or does it just store photos for you to compare manually?
  • Operator acknowledgement. Is there a mechanism (QR receipt, signature, scan) for the operator to acknowledge condition at the moment of transfer?
  • Free tier that actually works. Can you prove the workflow fits your life before paying, or is the free tier crippled to force an upgrade?

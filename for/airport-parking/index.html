Airport parking lots process thousands of vehicles daily, and cars sit unattended for days — making them one of the highest-risk environments for damage disputes. With CarShake, you give every parking customer the option to scan their vehicle on entry and rescan on exit, creating a timestamped, GPS-verified condition log for the entire stay. The QR handover receipt means both the customer and your lot attendant confirm the vehicle condition before the keys are handed over. This single workflow reduces damage claims filed against your lot, provides irrefutable evidence when claims do arise, and builds trust with travelers who are often anxious about leaving their car for days.

Airport parking operators also benefit from the operational data: scan counts by terminal, time-of-day patterns, and incident rates per lot. This intelligence helps managers allocate staffing, identify high-risk zones, and benchmark performance across locations. The CarShake QR-based workflow integrates with existing ticket-dispenser systems or runs standalone, and the driver-facing experience is deliberately simple — open the browser, scan, done. No app download, no account creation required, which means adoption rates are high even among one-time travelers. For operators concerned about liability exposure, one avoided claim pays for years of scans.

How Miami valet damage law works

Florida Statutes section 715.07 governs towing and vehicle storage, while common law bailment principles apply to valet damage claims.

In practice, this means the valet company is not automatically liable. You have to prove the damage occurred while they had control of your vehicle. That requires evidence: photos taken immediately before handover, a timestamped receipt, and ideally photos at pickup showing any new damage. Most Miami drivers do not have this evidence, which is exactly why valet damage claims get denied.

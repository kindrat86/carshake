Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade from Free to Premium anytime?

Yes. Upgrading is instant within the app. Any scans you already created on the free tier remain accessible and are included in your cloud storage on Premium.

What happens to my scans if I cancel Premium?

Your scans remain accessible on your device. Cloud-stored scans are retained for 30 days after cancellation so you can download them.

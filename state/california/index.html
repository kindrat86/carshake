Governing Bailment Law

California Civil Code §2110-2128 (gratuitous bailment) and §1851-1864 (bailment for hire)

Statute of Limitations

You have 3 years for breach of bailment (CCP §338) to file a claim for valet parking damage in California.

Notable Application

Strong bailment protections; valet parking is considered a bailment for hire, placing duty of reasonable care on the parking operator.

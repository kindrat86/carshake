What Is Diminished Value in Car Damage?

How diminished value claims work after vehicle damage.

Diminished value is the reduction in a car's market value after it has been damaged and repaired. Even after perfect repairs, a car with damage history is worth less.

Types of Diminished Value

  • Inherent: the automatic loss of value from having damage history
  • Repair-related: loss of value from imperfect repairs
  • Claim-related: loss of value from using aftermarket parts

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I claim diminished value after valet damage?

Yes. If your car is damaged by a valet, you can claim not only the repair cost but also the diminished value. This is especially relevant for luxury and newer vehicles.

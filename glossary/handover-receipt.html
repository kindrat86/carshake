What Is a Handover Receipt?

Definition: A digital or physical document that records the transfer of vehicle custody between two parties, noting the vehicle's condition at the moment of handover.

Understanding Handover Receipts

A handover receipt creates shared accountability. When both parties acknowledge the vehicle's condition at handover, post-hoc damage claims have no standing. QR-coded digital receipts are increasingly replacing paper forms.

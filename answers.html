How to document vehicle damage for valet
Use CarShake's three-stop protocol: photograph all sides before handing over keys, get the QR receipt, and photograph again on retrieval. Timestamps prove when damage occurred.
What to do if valet damages your car
If valet damages your car, present your CarShake pre-condition scan and post-retrieval scan. The timestamped evidence proves the damage occurred while the vehicle was in valet custody.
How to protect against false damage claims
Prevent false claims by scanning every vehicle before and after handover using CarShake. The cryptographic timestamps make it impossible to dispute when damage occurred.

How to use this letter

1. Scan your car with CarShake first

If you haven't already, run a CarShake scan to get timestamped, GPS-verified photos. Attach these to the letter — photo evidence is what wins claims.

2. Get a repair estimate

Visit a body shop for a written estimate. 1–2 estimates strengthen your claim. Attach them to the letter.

3. Send via certified mail

Send the letter via USPS certified mail with return receipt. This creates a paper trail proving they received it. Keep copies of everything.

4. Allow 14–30 days for response

Most legitimate claims are resolved within 30 days. If they deny or ignore you, the demand letter + evidence is your foundation for small claims court.

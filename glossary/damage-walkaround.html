Damage Walkaround

A systematic visual inspection of a vehicle's exterior to identify and document existing damage.

In depth

The damage walkaround is the most important step in any vehicle handover. It must be conducted in good lighting, follow a consistent pattern (typically clockwise from driver's front), and capture all pre-existing damage.

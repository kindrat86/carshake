Frequently Asked Questions

Should I file a police report for parking lot damage?

For damage over $500, it's advisable to file a police report. The report number is helpful for insurance claims. For minor damage under $500, a detailed parking lot damage report with photos is usually sufficient.

Does parking lot insurance cover valet damage?

Some parking garages have liability insurance that covers damage caused by their facility (e.g., falling objects, faulty equipment). Valet damage is typically covered by the valet operator's insurance, not the parking lot.

CarShake for Valet Operators

CarShake gives valet operators a 60-second, mutually confirmed record of every car's condition at drop-off and at return. When a guest claims new damage, you settle the question with timestamped evidence instead of a he-said-she-said argument.

Why valet operators use CarShake

Every valet stand lives one scratched bumper away from an angry guest, a chargeback, or a small-claims letter. The problem is almost never whether damage exists — it is whether the damage was there before the keys changed hands. A paper ticket with a damage diagram depends on the attendant's diligence and the guest's goodwill. CarShake replaces that with a photographic record both sides confirm on the spot.

Because the record is mutually acknowledged at drop-off, guests cannot claim pre-existing damage was yours, and your attendants cannot be accused of hiding anything. Most disputes never start, because there is nothing left to dispute.

Where it fits your operation

CarShake is a browser web app, so guests do not need to install anything — the attendant shows a QR code and the handshake takes seconds at the podium. It works for hotel valet, restaurant valet, event parking, and garage valet alike. Attendants need no special hardware beyond a phone.

How a CarShake handover works

  1. Scan before handover. A guided walk-around captures 8 angles of the vehicle in about 60 seconds. Every photo is GPS-stamped, server-timestamped, and SHA-256 hashed, so nobody can backdate or alter it.
  2. Confirm with a QR handshake. The other party scans a QR code and acknowledges the recorded condition. Both sides now hold the same evidence.
  3. Re-scan on return. A second walk-around captures the vehicle's condition at hand-back.
  4. Compare. AI-assisted comparison lines up the before and after scans and flags anything new. If there is a dispute, either side can export a court-ready PDF evidence report.

Frequently asked questions

Does scanning slow down the drop-off line?

A full 8-angle scan takes about 60 seconds. Busy stands scan while the guest finishes checking in, or scan only high-value vehicles as policy.

Do guests need to download an app?

No. CarShake runs in the browser. The guest scans a QR code and confirms the recorded condition — that is the whole flow.

What happens when a guest reports damage at pick-up?

You re-scan the car and compare against the drop-off record. The AI-assisted comparison flags what changed. Either side can export a PDF evidence report.

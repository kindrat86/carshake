Parking & Navigation Alternatives

CarShake vs BestParking

BestParking helps you find and book parking spots. CarShake protects your car once it's parked — with AI scans and QR handover receipts to document condition before valet.

CarShake vs SpotHero

SpotHero is a parking reservation platform. CarShake is a valet damage proof tool. Use both: SpotHero to find your spot, CarShake to document your car's condition before handing over the keys.

CarShake vs ParkWhiz

ParkWhiz offers parking reservations and discounts. CarShake provides AI-verified vehicle condition documentation at parking — complementary tools for different stages of the parking experience.

Vehicle History & Condition Tools

CarShake vs Carfax

Carfax compiles historical vehicle data — accident history, title issues, service records. CarShake documents present condition in real time with AI scans, GPS timestamping, and QR handover proof. Carfax tells you what happened; CarShake proves what's happening now.

CarShake vs Damage iD

Damage iD is a vehicle condition documentation tool for transfers. CarShake adds valet-specific workflow, QR-coded receipts, time-bound custody chains, and consumer mobile simplicity — all free.

Ride & Delivery Platforms

CarShake vs Uber

Uber moves people. CarShake protects parked cars. When you drive yourself and use valet, CarShake gives you the evidence Uber can't — documented vehicle condition at handover.

CarShake vs Postmates

Postmates delivers goods. CarShake delivers proof. Different products entirely — but both save you money in their respective domains.

Fleet & Enterprise Tools

CarShake vs Fleetio

Fleetio is fleet maintenance software for businesses managing multiple vehicles. CarShake is for individual drivers who want valet damage proof — free, no enterprise contract, no fleet manager required.

CarShake vs VALET

VALET focuses on parking management and operations. CarShake focuses on protecting the driver — AI scans, QR receipts, and court-ready evidence before and after valet handover.

CarShake vs Zum

Zum provides student transportation and fleet management for schools. CarShake protects individual drivers at valet parking. Different audiences, different problems.

Luxury & Specialized Protection

CarShake vs Luxre

Luxre is a luxury vehicle protection service. CarShake is free and works for any car — from daily drivers to exotics — with the same AI-verified, GPS-timestamped, court-admissible evidence.

CarShake vs Valet Anywhere

Valet Anywhere is an on-demand valet service. CarShake is a valet damage proof tool. Use Valet Anywhere for the convenience; use CarShake for the documentation if something goes wrong.

Why CarShake Is Different

Most alternatives are parking finders, fleet tools, or vehicle history databases. CarShake is the only tool specifically built for the valet handover moment — the 60 seconds when you hand your keys to a stranger. The 3-Stop Protocol (scan → QR receipt → rescan with AI comparison) creates an unbroken chain of custody that none of the alternatives provide.

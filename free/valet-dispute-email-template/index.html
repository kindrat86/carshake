Frequently Asked Questions

Should I call or email first?

Email first—it creates a written record of your communication. Phone calls are useful for follow-up, but always confirm any verbal agreements in writing via email.

How long should I wait for a response?

Most valet companies respond within 7-14 days. If you haven't heard back in 14 days, send a follow-up email and consider alternative resolution methods.

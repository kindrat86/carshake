About CarShake

CarShake is a valet damage proof app founded in 2026. CarShake is a free valet-damage-proof and vehicle-handover app that scans and time-stamps a car's condition before and after handover, giving drivers, valet operators, and rental fleets court-admissible proof to defeat false damage claims.

Our Mission

We build tools for drivers, valet operators, rental fleets, car dealerships, body shops, and parking facilities. Our focus: the free valet-damage-proof & car-handover app..

What We Do

CarShake is a free valet-damage-proof and vehicle-handover app that scans and time-stamps a car's condition before and after handover, giving drivers, valet operators, and rental fleets court-admissible proof to defeat false damage claims.

Why We Built This

We saw a gap in the market for a purpose-built tool serving drivers, valet operators, rental fleets, car dealerships, body shops, and parking facilities. Existing solutions were either too generic or too expensive. CarShake fills that gap.

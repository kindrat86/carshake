CarShake for Body Shops

CarShake lets a body shop photograph and timestamp a vehicle's full condition at intake — before anyone touches it — and again at delivery. Claims that the shop caused a scratch, curb rash, or interior mark are answered with the intake record the customer confirmed.

Why body shops use CarShake

Every estimator knows the pattern: the car arrives for a rear bumper respray, and at pick-up the customer notices — for the first time — a wheel scuff on the other side. Now the shop is negotiating goodwill work on damage it never caused, because it cannot prove the scuff predates the repair.

A CarShake intake scan fixes the car's condition at the moment of drop-off: 8 angles, GPS-stamped, server-timestamped, SHA-256 hashed, and confirmed by the customer with a QR handshake. The delivery re-scan closes the loop. What changed in between is your work; what did not is on the record.

Where it fits your operation

Run the scan at the estimate bay or the drop-off lane. It is a browser app, so writers use any phone — no hardware, no install for the customer. Shops that photograph intake condition already can keep their process and add the parts photos alone cannot provide: trusted timestamps and the customer's confirmation.

How a CarShake handover works

  1. Scan before handover. A guided walk-around captures 8 angles of the vehicle in about 60 seconds. Every photo is GPS-stamped, server-timestamped, and SHA-256 hashed, so nobody can backdate or alter it.
  2. Confirm with a QR handshake. The other party scans a QR code and acknowledges the recorded condition. Both sides now hold the same evidence.
  3. Re-scan on return. A second walk-around captures the vehicle's condition at hand-back.
  4. Compare. AI-assisted comparison lines up the before and after scans and flags anything new. If there is a dispute, either side can export a court-ready PDF evidence report.

Frequently asked questions

We already take intake photos. Why is this better?

Loose photos prove what a car looked like, not when, and the customer never agreed to them. CarShake adds server timestamps, hashes, and a customer confirmation step.

Does the customer have to be present?

The QR confirmation is done from the customer's own phone. For tow-ins or after-hours drop-offs, scan at intake and send the record link for review.

Can I export the record if a dispute escalates?

Yes. Either party can export a PDF evidence report containing the photos and their timestamps.

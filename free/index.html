Common Questions

Are these car tools really free?

Yes. All eight CarShake free tools are 100% free with no signup, no credit card, and no watermark. Use them for personal, rental, or commercial vehicles.

Do I need to install an app?

No. Every tool runs in your browser. There is nothing to install. You can also install the CarShake web app for QR handover receipts and time-stamped cloud proof.

Can I use these for rental cars and valet disputes?

Yes. The rental car inspection checklist, valet damage claim letter, and valet dispute email template are built for exactly these situations. Combined with time-stamped photos they produce court-admissible documentation.

Do you store my photos or data?

No. Calculations and document generation happen in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers or stored.

How to Document Car Damage Properly

The definitive guide to creating court-admissible vehicle damage documentation.

Pre-handover documentation

Before handing over your keys, photograph all four sides of the vehicle plus close-ups of any existing damage. Use a tool like CarShake for timestamped, GPS-tagged photos.

Post-handover inspection

Inspect the vehicle immediately upon return. Walk around systematically and photograph any new damage with reference points.

Writing the incident report

Document the date, time, location, valet attendant name, and specific damage. Be factual and detailed. Avoid emotional language.

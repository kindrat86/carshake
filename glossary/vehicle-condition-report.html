Vehicle Condition Report (VCR)

A documented record of a vehicle's physical state at a specific point in time.

In depth

A VCR is the foundational document for any vehicle handover, whether valet, rental, or purchase. It protects all parties by establishing a baseline condition. Without a timestamped, photo-backed VCR, damage disputes become he-said-she-said arguments.

The honest pricing breakdown

CarShake pricing is deliberately simple because the value proposition is deliberately simple. The free tier is genuinely free — not a crippled trial designed to push you to paid. You get 3 full scans, each of which is a complete 8-angle guided walkaround with time-stamped, geo-located photos and the before/after diff workflow. That is enough to document a full round-trip handover (one pre-scan, one post-scan, and one to spare) and prove the workflow fits your life before you ever enter a credit card.

The paid tier is the Founding tier at $2.97/month, locked forever for founding members. Founding spots are limited — the free tier is available at the time of writing — and the founding price does not increase as features are added. Founding members get unlimited scans, the full before/after diff library, and priority export options for claims and disputes.
